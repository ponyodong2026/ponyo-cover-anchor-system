# Sunlit Scrapbook Cutout Style

Use this file when the user wants a warm, realistic, lifestyle-photo Xiaohongshu cover with a tilted top photo strip, a large subject with thick white hand-drawn outline, oversized handwritten brush title, and soft yellow/green keyword highlights.

Chinese name: `阳光胶片拼贴白描边风`.

This style is for finished covers, not article illustrations. The output should look like a real social-media cover, not a web layout or a poster explaining the article.

## Style Contract

- Canvas: `3:4 vertical social-media cover, 1080x1440`
- Base: warm real-life photography, golden-hour sunlight, shallow depth of field
- Color: cream / olive / brown base, with butter yellow and sage green keyword highlights
- Top strip: 18-25% of the canvas, tilted photo-strip collage with 2-4 panels
- Panel borders: rough, uneven, white, hand-drawn, like taped photo cutouts
- Main subject: right or lower-right, about 42-58% of canvas, with thick irregular white hand-drawn outline
- Title: left side, oversized handwritten brush Chinese typography, 3-5 short chunks
- Decor: sparse doodle arrows, sparkles, hearts, dashed path, small sun icon, casual underlines
- Topics: travel / Citywalk / Vlog / outfit / skincare / beauty / home workout / wellness / lifestyle routine

## Watermark Policy

Do not add `波妞`, `PONYO`, logo marks, creator signatures, `@` handles, or any hard-coded author watermark to generated cover images.

If the user explicitly provides their own account name or signature, include only that user-provided mark. Otherwise output a clean cover with no watermark.

## Why This Style Works

- The top photo strip gives proof, story, and detail before the viewer reads.
- The thick white cutout outline separates the person/product from a busy photo background.
- The handwritten brush title feels personal and native to lifestyle feeds.
- Butter-yellow and sage-green keywords create a soft but obvious hook.

## Universal Prompt

```text
Create a finished 3:4 vertical social-media cover poster, 1080x1440 style.
Style: sunlit scrapbook cutout cover, warm real-life lifestyle photo collage, handwritten brush typography.

Topic: <topic>.
Audience: <audience>.

Background:
Warm golden-hour lifestyle photography, shallow depth of field, cream olive brown color grading, soft natural backlight, realistic photo texture.

Composition:
- Top 20% tilted photo-strip collage with 3 panels: <panel 1>, <panel 2>, <panel 3>. Each panel has rough uneven white hand-drawn borders, like taped scrapbook photos.
- Main subject: <person/product/scene>, placed on the right side, occupying 45-55% of the canvas, with thick irregular white hand-drawn cutout outline.
- Left side reserved for oversized handwritten brush Chinese title.

Exact readable Chinese copy:
- Main headline: "<headline>"
- Supporting line: "<support line>"
- Small note: "<optional tiny note>"

Typography:
Large casual handwritten brush Chinese title, chalky texture, white base text with butter-yellow and sage-green keyword highlights. The biggest keyword or number is the main visual anchor. Supporting line is smaller white handwritten text with a casual underline.

Decor:
Small doodle sparkles, hearts, arrows, dashed path, sun icon, and casual underlines. Keep them sparse and hand-drawn.

Constraints:
No watermark, no creator signature, no logo, no @ handle unless the user provides one. No QR code, no random extra words, no fake platform UI. Chinese text must be crisp, legible, correctly written. Do not cover the face, product, or key action. Keep top strip details visible but secondary.
```

## Failure Modes

- The top strip becomes too tall and steals attention from the title.
- The handwritten title becomes too small or too decorative to read.
- The white outline is too clean and vector-like; it should look hand-drawn.
- The doodles become clutter and reduce the premium lifestyle feeling.
- The background is too dark; this style needs warm sunlight and visible real-life texture.
- The prompt asks for too many text snippets; keep one headline, one support line, and at most one tiny note.
